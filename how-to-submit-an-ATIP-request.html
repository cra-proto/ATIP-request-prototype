Access to information and privacy at the Canada Revenue Agency

How to send an ATIP request

ATIP Online

This account allows you to send ATIP requests to any government institution that is subject to the Access to Information Act or the Privacy Act, including the CRA.

When you create an account, you can:

  • Receive a copy of your records electronically
  • Access the status of your requests
  • Communicate directly with the Access to Information and Privacy Directorate

  • I have an ATIP online account: Option 1

    Send an ATIP request

  • I want to create an account: Option 2

    Create an ATIP online account

By mail

  1. Complete Form RC378, Access to Information and Personal Information Request, or write a letter that contains all of the necessary information about your request
  2. Mail your completed form to:

    Director General, ATIP Directorate
    Canada Revenue Agency
    555 MacKenzie Avenue, 5th Floor
    Ottawa ON  K1A 0L5
